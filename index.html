I am a graphic designer, collector, classifier, drawing practitioner, and artist. I hold an academic background in Visual Communication Design and Oriental Painting. My practice explores the intersection of drawing, language, and systems of organization. I have held solo and group exhibitions both online and offline, covering themes ranging from typographic practice to curatorial experimentation. As the founder of NOWWE Publishing and Sculpture Poetry, I create artist books that function as sculptural and poetic objects. My design work includes illustration and graphic design for publications, symposiums, and cultural platforms. Through publishing, exhibitions, and workshops, I investigate how visual structures—such as classification systems, typographic forms, and printed matter—can carry personal, collective, and poetic meaning.
